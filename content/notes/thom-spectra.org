These are some notes on Thom spectra, placed within the context of the six-functor formalism
of parametrised spectra. They are based on a Chinese version _[[https://www.bananaspace.org/wiki/%E7%94%A8%E6%88%B7:NaCNO/%E5%88%9D%E8%AF%86Thom%E6%9E%84%E9%80%A0][on 香蕉空间]]_ that I drafted during
the summer of 2025, which I translated to English and expanded quite a bit around December. There
was a TeXed up Chinese version before the content expansion, which I might make public after
backporting the changes (when I have time and remember).

[[/files/thom-spectra-intro-english.pdf][Link to the notes (PDF)]]

The ideas are mostly based on the work of _[[https://arxiv.org/abs/1403.4325][Ando--Blumberg--Gepner--Hopkins--Rezk]]_.
I should mention the work of _[[https://arxiv.org/abs/1411.7988][Antolín-Camarena--Barthel]]_ as well, which hasn't come up in the notes.
The notes are more centered around the use of six-functors for classical algebraic topology.

The current notes have been distributed within our working groups and to a few fellow students,
but not more broadly than that, as I have hesitations about conducting much academic activity
under my current legal identity, especially on the internet.
